Privacy Policy

Guapa Data — Last updated: March 21, 2026

The Short Version

We collect very little data. If you submit the contact form on our Data Solutions page, we receive your name, email, and message. If you browse the site without submitting a form, we don't collect any personal information. We don't sell or share your data with anyone.

What We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

Data Solutions contact form. If you submit the lead capture form on our Data Solutions page, we collect your name, email address, and the content of your message. This form is processed through Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party form handling service. Your submission is transmitted to Formspree's servers and forwarded to us via email. Formspree's own privacy policy governs their handling of your data during transmission and storage on their platform.

Newsletter signup. If and when newsletter functionality is activated, we will collect your email address for the sole purpose of sending occasional updates about Guapa Data. You may unsubscribe at any time.

Information Collected Automatically

We do not run any analytics tracking software on this site. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other tracking or analytics tools. We do not set cookies.

GitHub Pages hosting. This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect basic server logs (IP addresses, browser type, referring pages) as part of their standard hosting infrastructure. This data is governed by GitHub's privacy policy and is not accessible to Guapa Data.

Information Collected by Third Parties Through This Site

When you use this site, your browser may make requests to third-party servers in the normal course of loading page content:

Cover Art Archive (coverartarchive.org / archive.org). Album artwork displayed on the music page is loaded directly from Cover Art Archive servers hosted by the Internet Archive. When these images load, your browser sends a standard HTTP request to their servers, which may include your IP address, browser type, and referring page. Guapa Data does not control or have access to this data. This is governed by the Internet Archive's privacy policy.

Amazon (amazon.com). If you click an affiliate link on this site, you will be directed to Amazon.com. Amazon will process your visit and any subsequent purchases under their own privacy policy. Guapa Data receives only aggregate reporting from Amazon Associates (total clicks, total earnings) and does not receive any personal information about individual purchasers.

Spotify (open.spotify.com). If you click a Spotify link on this site, you will be directed to Spotify's platform. Guapa Data does not receive any data about your Spotify activity.

Wikipedia (wikipedia.org). If you click a Wikipedia link, you will be directed to Wikipedia's site under their own privacy policy.

How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through the Data Solutions contact form is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry about Guapa Data Solutions products and services
  • Follow up with relevant information about consulting engagements, product availability, or service offerings

We do not use your information for any other purpose.

What We Don't Do

  • We don't sell your personal information to anyone
  • We don't share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes
  • We don't use cookies or tracking technologies
  • We don't build user profiles or track behavior across sessions
  • We don't serve targeted advertising
  • We don't use your data to train AI models

Data Storage and Security

Contact form submissions are transmitted through Formspree and delivered to us via email. We do not maintain a separate customer database at this time. Formspree stores submission data on their servers; their data handling practices are described in their privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and maintain a record of our communications. If you would like your information deleted, contact us and we will remove it within 30 days.

Your Rights

Regardless of where you are located, you may:

  • Request access to any personal information we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Withdraw consent for any future communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us through the Data Solutions form or our social channels listed on the site. We will respond within 30 days.

California Residents

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Given our minimal data collection practices, the scope of these rights is limited, but you are entitled to know what personal information we collect and to request its deletion. We do not sell personal information as defined by the CCPA.

European Visitors

If you are located in the European Economic Area, your personal data is protected under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Our lawful basis for processing contact form submissions is your consent, which you provide by voluntarily submitting the form. You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us.

We do not actively target or market to EU residents, but we respect the rights of any EU visitor who interacts with our site. If you wish to exercise any GDPR rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability, or objection), contact us and we will respond within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information through our contact form, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted to this page. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be directed to the contact form on the Data Solutions page or by reaching out through our social channels listed on the site.
